Snickerdoodle in a Jar

Thank you for joining me at my bachelorette party!

  1. Place the cinnamon mix cup in a small bowl and set aside
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. In a large bowl, cream 1/2 cup of butter and 1 egg.
  4. Pour in the snickerdoodle mix, and stir until a dough forms.
  5. Roll the dough into 1 inch balls, roll the balls in the cinnamon-sugar mixture, and place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 to 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Cookies should be light brown. Cool on wire racks.
